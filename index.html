COMPUTER SOCIETY OF INDIA
DTU CHAPTER

A society for the geeks. A society for the developers. A society for the gamers. A society for public relations. Most importantly, a society for those who wish to learn.

For the beginners

Regular sessions and IDGs are held for C++, Java and python. Concepts are taught from the scratch. No prior knowledge is required. Classes are taken ny the seniors who have spent years learning the languages.

Competitive Programming

Our IDGs for Algorithms, Data-Structures and Algorithms are also very famous.Recursion, dynamic programming, backtracking all are discussed in our session, giving the learner to strengthen his/her grip on competitive programming.

Development

The field of development is not at all unknown to us. Sessions for web development are our most sought out sessions. Seniors who have developed and published their own websites and apps take the classes and instill the skill of development in the students.

Our Events

We organise many events in the whole academic year. The october fest and others are organised on a small scale, while the FEB TECHNICAL FEST is the grandest of all. Events that are often the part of our ventures are

LAN GAMING

The most popular event is organised twice or thrice in an year. Gamers come and compete on FIFA 15, Counter Strike, Age of Empires, NFS and many more. Competitions for teams and individuals both are organised. COME, PLAY and WIN.

CODING COMPETITION

This is the place to show off your skills and win prizes. Online rounds, offline quiz and on-site coding rounds are organized by out hardworking team. A lot of people come but a few make to the finals. And the best take the big prizes.

GK QUIZ

For those who are proud of their General Knowledge or those who wished they were historians, or those who are feel they are good at bollywood and political data. Come and give the quiz. Check if your knowledge deserves a prize or you are one of those who bark but not bite.

HACKATHONS

Hackathons! The one where you spend hours and hours continuously to develop something unique, something that you can show the world and something that the world feels proud about you.
